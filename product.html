What is a Smart Meter?
A smart meter is a digital device that measures electricity usage in real time. Unlike traditional meters, it provides accurate data, enabling better energy management and helping users reduce electricity costs.
Real-time energy monitoring
Digital and accurate readings
Displays voltage, current, power, and energy
Supports energy efficiency and cost reduction
